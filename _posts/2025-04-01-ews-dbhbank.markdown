Architected and deployed an enhanced defaulter warning system for DBH Bank focusing on individual clients. Implemented predictive models using XGBoost, Linear Regression, and Prophet for time-series forecasting to identify potential financial risks. Transitioned frontend from Streamlit to Vue.js for improved scalability. Successfully deployed to VPS infrastructure ensuring robust production environment and continuous availability for bank operations.
